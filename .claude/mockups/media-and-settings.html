Why these are utility surfaces, not nav items

Media and Settings sit behind header icons rather than the primary nav because daily use doesn't require them. The clinic owner spends time on Home and Stories; they visit Media when adding a hero image to a draft and Settings only when something needs configuring. Keeping the primary nav at 2 items makes everything else feel like deliberate navigation, not background noise.

Cross-surface link: the "used ×N" badge on every interview-derived media asset jumps you back to the Story Detail page that consumed it — so Media isn't a graveyard, it's an index into your stories.
