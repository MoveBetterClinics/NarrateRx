# Free Public Seminar Campaign — Operating Plan

_Last updated: 2026-05-21_

## The funnel

**Instagram paid ad → GoHighLevel landing page → Seminar attendance → Consult booking → Patient**

Each tool has one job:

- **GoHighLevel** = the operational machine. Landing page, registration form, calendar event, email + SMS reminders, attendance tracking, post-event nurture pipeline, attribution back to ad spend.
- **NarrateRx** = the voice-true content fuel. Every social post, email excerpt, and video script Michael creates from his interviews automatically points readers to the seminar RSVP page — in his own voice, with the invitation sentence we wrote, linking to the exact GHL URL.

The point of the pairing: the paid ad gets you the cold audience, but organic content from Michael's interviews **lowers cost-per-registrant** over the quarter because his social/email/video posts also drive RSVPs without paid spend.

## How NarrateRx supports this (new this week)

**Workspace-wide default**: Admin sets the workspace's content focus. Default stays on **Bookings** — everyone's day-to-day content drives consult bookings as normal.

**Per-clinician override**: Each clinician (currently 5 + 1 intern across 2 clinics) can override their own content focus from their own clinician profile page. For the seminar push:

- **Michael** sets his override to **Seminars** with the RSVP URL + invitation sentence + button text + event date
- **Everyone else** leaves their override off → their content stays on Bookings
- Result: Michael drives the seminar; the other clinicians' content keeps acquiring patients normally. No seminar overload in the feed.

**What flows where**:

| Content type | Carries the campaign CTA? |
|---|---|
| Blog post (the keystone long-form piece) | **No — evergreen by design** |
| Social posts (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, GBP, Threads, etc.) | Yes — uses the override of whichever clinician the interview belongs to |
| Email / newsletter excerpts | Yes — same rule |
| Video scripts | Yes — same rule |

Blog posts stay timeless so they keep working as evergreen SEO assets. Only the derivative pieces (social/email/video) carry the seminar CTA, and only for clinicians who've opted in.

## What changes for the team

### For Michael (or whoever's hosting the seminar)
- Settings ⚙ → My clinician profile → Set personal override → Seminars → paste the GHL RSVP link + invitation sentence + date
- Every new social post, email excerpt, and video script generated from your interviews will reference the seminar and link to the RSVP page

### For everyone else (clinicians + intern)
- Nothing changes day-to-day
- Your content continues driving consult bookings as it does today
- If you want to also push the seminar from your interviews, set your own override the same way

### For Drq (admin)
- The workspace default is now admin-only to change — prevents accidental flips
- Manage at Settings ⚙ → Mode chip (or Workspace Settings)

## Phases for the quarter

1. **Pre-launch** (this week): Build GHL landing page + reminder workflows. Michael sets his NarrateRx override.
2. **Ad live + organic amplification** (4–6 weeks before each seminar): IG ad runs; Michael's interviews generate seminar-CTA'd social/email/video that compounds the paid reach.
3. **Day of**: GHL handles check-in + attendance tagging.
4. **Post-seminar** (10 days): Quick Michael debrief interview → NarrateRx generates the follow-up email sequence + an "if you missed it" recap that doubles as ad creative for the next seminar.
5. **Quarter loop**: Report on cost-per-registrant, cost-per-attendee, cost-per-consult-booked.

## Quick reference for the staff

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| "Will my Instagram posts get hijacked to push the seminar?" | No — only if you set your personal override. Default behavior is unchanged. |
| "Do I have to do anything?" | No, unless you want to actively push the seminar from your interviews. |
| "What about blogs?" | Blogs are evergreen. They never carry the seminar CTA. |
| "Where do I set my override?" | Settings ⚙ → My clinician profile → Set personal override. |
| "Can I change my mind?" | Yes — toggle override off at any time and your next drafts revert to the workspace default. |
| "Who can change the workspace-wide default?" | Admins only. |
