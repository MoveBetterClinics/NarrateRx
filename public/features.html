Features

Every part is built around one rule: your voice is the input.

Below is what's already in the app, what's in active development for the founding cohort, and what's coming next quarter. Founding owners get every feature in this list, automatically, as it ships.

Live in the app

What ships today.

Live

Prompted interview recorder

In-app recording with smart prompt sequencing. Pause, re-record, or finish on your phone later. Up to 30-minute sessions, transcribed in under a minute.

Live

Word-for-word transcript

Speaker-labeled, timestamped, fully editable. Fix a misheard name once and every downstream draft updates.

Live

Four-channel drafting

Blog, social (6 variants), email, and Google Business Profile — all generated in parallel from the same interview, in your voice.

Live

Review queue

Approve, reject, or schedule each draft. Inline edit headlines and pull quotes. NarrateRx learns from what you cut.

Live

Buffer publish integration

One-click queue every social channel + Google Business Profile via your existing Buffer account. Founding-cohort priority.

Live

Native WordPress + Astro publish

Push straight to your real site with images, internal links, and SEO metadata applied. No copy-paste tour.

Live

AI-generated hero images

Cover art tuned to your brand palette, plus 1080×1080 social variants. Stops the "stock photo that looks like every other clinic" problem.

Live

Media library

Every interview, transcript, draft, and image you've ever generated, tagged, searchable, and reusable across future posts.

Live

Brand kit settings

Voice rules, internal link library, audience description, hashtag set, and CTA URL — set once, applied to every draft automatically.

Voice-faithful tuning

Per-clinician phrase library. NarrateRx tracks the exact phrases you accept and reject, then preserves your stylistic fingerprints automatically.

In dev

Content calendar view

Drag-and-drop month view of every queued draft. See what's going out when, swap order, hold a piece for next month.

In dev

Engagement loop

Per-post engagement read from Google Analytics, Meta, and TrustDrivenCare. Surfaces what's working so the next month's prompts double down.

Open-mic conversational mode — the model asks follow-ups based on what you just said, instead of running through a static script.

In dev

Multi-clinician workspaces

Add team members, separate review queues per clinician, shared brand kit. Each voice tracked independently for tuning.

Multi-location publishing

For practices with more than one location: per-location GBP queue, location keyword injection, distinct review channels.
