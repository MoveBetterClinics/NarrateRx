The AI's job

The AI is the assembler. Not the author.

Every other tool in this space scrapes your website, ingests a few posts, and asks an LLM to be you. The output is fluent and forgettable, because there's nothing actually in it. NarrateRx works the other way: your words go in, the AI just shapes them.

What the AI does

  • Transcribes your 15-minute interview, word for word
  • Sorts your ideas into email, social, and Google Business drafts
  • Shapes each draft to the format — length, structure, hook
  • Generates supporting media so you don't pay a designer for hero images

What the AI doesn't do

  • Invent ideas. If you didn't say it, it isn't in the draft.
  • Scrape your website to fake your voice
  • Decide your point of view — that part is already yours
  • Write the month if you skip the interview
  • Pretend to be the expert. You are.

That's why the output sounds like you. Because it is you.
