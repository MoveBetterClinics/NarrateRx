The bottleneck

Clinicians know things their patients need to hear.

The expertise isn't the problem. The translation is. Between full schedules, charting, and the hundred small fires of running a practice, the writing never gets done — and the patients who'd benefit most never hear the answer.

What it does

One conversation. A week of content.

01

Conversational interview

NarrateRx asks the questions a sharp marketer would — about the patient story, the mechanism, the misconception. You answer like you'd talk to a patient on the table.

02

Multi-channel output

One interview becomes a long-form blog post, an email newsletter, an Instagram carousel, a LinkedIn post, and short-form scripts — each tuned to the platform, not copy-pasted.

03

Brand-true voice

Configured per practice. Your tone, your clinical language, your authority — preserved across every output, every clinician, every week.

04

Review, schedule, publish

Edit in place, regenerate sections you want sharper, schedule into your queue. The clinician's job ends at the interview.
