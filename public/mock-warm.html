How it actually works

One pipeline. Three honest steps.

No prompt engineering, no template tuning, no agency briefs. NarrateRx is the system around a single architectural decision: the recording is the source of truth.

01 — Speak

Talk for fifteen minutes

NarrateRx prompts you with the right questions — the case that stuck with you, the belief most peers don't share, the thing patients ask over and over. You answer out loud. That's the work.

02 — Draft

The month writes itself

One transcript becomes a long-form blog post, six social captions, an email newsletter, and a Google Business update. Every word traceable back to a moment in your interview.

03 — Ship

One click, every channel

Connect Buffer once. The review queue handles approvals; publishing handles itself. Thirty minutes a week, all in, replaces an agency retainer.
