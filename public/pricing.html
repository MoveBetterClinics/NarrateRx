Pricing questions

Things people ask first.

How can it really be free?
Founding owners are doing us a favor. They're surfacing the rough edges, telling us which features matter, and validating that NarrateRx works outside Move Better. That kind of feedback is worth more to us right now than the monthly fee would be. The founding cohort caps at ten so we can actually do this thoughtfully.
What's the catch?
You have to do the interview each month. That's the whole product — if you skip the interview, there's nothing to draft. And we'll ask you for feedback periodically. That's it. No data resale, no retainer obligation, no auto-renew when paid tiers launch.
What does "founding rate locked in for life" actually mean?
When paid tiers launch, every founding cohort member will receive an email with a one-click "lock my founding rate" link. The locked rate will be substantially below the public Owner-Operator tier. You keep that rate as long as you stay subscribed. If you cancel and rejoin later, you re-enter at whatever the going public rate is.
Will the price be reasonable when it launches?
Our target for the Owner-Operator tier is around $79/month — well below the $400–$1,200/month a content agency charges. Founding rate will be below that. We're not building a Pro / Enterprise / Quote-Us pricing page; this is a small-clinic tool that should cost what a small-clinic tool costs.
Do I sign a contract?
No. Founding cohort is a handshake. You apply, we talk, you onboard, you keep using it for as long as you want. If you walk, you keep the drafts.
