Pricing questions

Things people ask first.

How can it really be free?
Founding members are doing us a favor. They surface the rough edges, tell us which features matter, and prove NarrateRx works outside Move Better. That feedback is worth more to us right now than the monthly fee would be. We keep the founding group small so we can do this thoughtfully.
What's the catch?
You have to do the interview each month. That's the whole product — if you skip the interview, there's nothing to draft. And we'll ask you for feedback periodically. That's it. No data resale, no retainer obligation, no auto-renew when paid tiers launch.
What happens to pricing when it starts?
Regular pricing starts in July 2026, and it's right above on this page — Solo $149, Practice $299, Multi-location $499 a month. Founding members keep a locked founding rate and get a direct heads-up at least 30 days before any change.
Is the price reasonable?
Solo starts at $149/month — well below the $400–$1,200/month a content agency charges, and far less than your own time is worth. There's no "Enterprise / Quote-Us" tier hiding the real number. It's a small-clinic tool priced like one.
Do I sign a contract?
No. Founding cohort is a handshake. You apply, we talk, you onboard, you keep using it for as long as you want. If you walk, you keep the drafts.
