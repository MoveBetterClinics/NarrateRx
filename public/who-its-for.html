Who it's for

Built for owner-operators whose voice is the moat.

NarrateRx isn't for the brand-led business with a 30-person marketing team. It's for the practitioner whose patients show up because of them — and who'd grow ten times faster if the world heard what their clients already do.

Verticals we've shipped

Same engine. Different voice prompts.

Every workspace is configured with its own prompt set, audience description, and link library — set up during onboarding, not hardcoded. The interview questions, tone targets, and hashtags shift to match what your audience actually responds to. The four below are the verticals already live; others are onboardable.

MC

Movement clinicians

Chiropractors, PTs, sports-med, and performance coaches whose patients have already tried "rest and ibuprofen." You think in movement, not tissue. You explain breathing, bracing, and load to people who've been told their disc is the problem.

  • Prompt set tuned for case stories and movement-first beliefs
  • Internal link library wired to your real site map
  • Subject lines that don't sound like an MLM supplement pitch
  • Currently shipping at Move Better, Portland, OR
EQ

Equine practitioners

Mobile equine chiropractors and bodyworkers. Your audience is owners and trainers who notice subtle signs — refused leads, tail swishing, posture shifts — and want evidence-informed, complementary care.

  • Subtle-signs vocabulary built into the prompts
  • "Complementary to veterinary care" framing on every draft
  • Discipline-specific examples (dressage, jumping, eventing, ranch)
  • Currently shipping at Move Better Equine, Ridgefield, WA
AC

Animal chiro & bodywork

AVCA-certified animal practitioners. Your audience is pet owners whose dogs slow on the stairs and whose cats are stiff getting off the couch — people who want a less invasive first option before surgery or long-term meds.

  • Owner-centered framing — "your dog who can't get up the stairs"
  • Avoids "fur baby" / "spa for pets" tropes by default
  • Working-dog and senior-pet vocabulary built in
  • Currently shipping at Move Better Animals, Portland + Vancouver
CC

Coaches & consultants

You sell access to a method. Business coaches, performance coaches, executive consultants. Your buyers vet you for thinking, not packaging. Your LinkedIn presence is your top-of-funnel.

  • Prompt set built around frameworks, beliefs, and client cases
  • LinkedIn-first output mode (long-form text > listicle)
  • Workshop / cohort CTA support out of the box
  • Founding cohort applications open — see if your method fits

An honest filter — who it's not for.

We'd rather lose the deal than burn six months of your time. If any of these describe you, NarrateRx is a bad fit and we'll happily tell you so.

  • You don't have a point of view yet — you're still figuring out your positioning.
  • Your marketing is "best service, friendly staff, great prices."
  • You won't do the 15-minute interview each month. Without that, there's nothing to draft.
  • You want fully automated content — no review, no edits, set-and-forget.
  • You're a venture-scale platform with a 20-person marketing team. We're built for owner-operators.
